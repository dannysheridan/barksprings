## Septic System

<Note>Please flush **toilet paper only**. All other items (including wipes, feminine products, and paper towels) should go in the trash.</Note>

## Rainwater

The house runs entirely on rainwater. You can use everything normally, but please be mindful of water usage.

### Turning Water ON (On Arrival)

In the storage closet through the laundry room, plug in both the water heater and the hot water circulator.

### Shutting Water OFF (On Departure)

In the storage closet through the laundry room, unplug both the water heater and the hot water circulator.

## Garbage Disposal

Only put soft foods down the disposal. Do not put eggshells, bones, or other hard items down the drain.

## Outdoor Shower

To use the outdoor shower, turn on the water under the sink in the kid's bathroom.

## For Guests Staying May-October

The water system will have been shut off for the summer season, so you'll need to re-establish consistent water level and pressure for the house.

<Steps>

    <Step>
    Follow all the steps above to turn the water on inside the house.
    </Step>

    <Step>
    Walk to the water tank and locate the green lid with an orange stake next to it. Turn the red knob to the closed position.
    </Step>

</Steps>

<Warning>When you leave, turn the knob back to the open position. Any remaining water in the system will flush out through the pipe.</Warning>

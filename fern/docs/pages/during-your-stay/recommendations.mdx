### **Car Rentals**

Highly recommended — rideshares takes **15–20 minutes** to show up. Austin airport has on-site rental car center, it’s a fairly painless process. 

### **Nearest Stores**

- Grocery - H-E-B is 10 min away, it has everything)
- Home Depot + Walgreens also 10 min away in Dripping Springs

### **Restaurants**

Casual

- Salt Lick for BBQ
- Crepe Crazy for lunch
- Pieous for lunch for dinner

Fancy

- Tillies at Camp Lucy (20min), make a reservation

**Activities**

Active

- Tennis, Pickleball, or Padel at Dripping Springs Racquet Club
  - Rackets, paddles, and balls are located in the entryway cabinets
- Hiking
  - Reimers Ranch (20min) has water, wildflowers, and rolling hills
  - Zilker Park and Barton Creek Greenbelt (30min, downtown Austin) are popular areas and have water features and lots of shade on the trails
  - Perdenales State Park (30min) need to make a reservation, outstanding trails
- Horseback Riding
  - Trail ride at XLR8 Ranch
- Rock Climbing
  - Book with Texas Climbing Adventures, at Reimers Ranch, great experience

Drinking

- Driftwood Estate Winery (5min), good vibes, no kids or dogs allowed, very average wine, food on the weekends, great views
- Deep Eddys Vodka (15min), outdoor or indoor activity, flight of flavors or regular drinks, fun vibes, no food
- Subs Monkey Brewery (20min), kid friendly, beer and pizza

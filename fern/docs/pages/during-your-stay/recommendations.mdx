### **Car Rentals**

Highly recommended — rideshares takes **15–20 minutes** to show up. Austin airport has on-site rental car center, it’s a fairly painless process. 

### **Nearest Stores**

- Grocery - H-E-B is 10 min away, it has everything)
- Home Depot 10 min away in Dripping Springs
- Walgreens (10 min)

### **Restaurants**

Casual

- Salt Lick for BBQ
- Crepe Crazy or lunch
- Pieous for lunch for dinner

Fancy

- Tillies at Camp Lucy (20 min), make a reservation

**Activities**

Active

- Tennis, Pickleball, or Padel at Dripping Springs Racquet Club
  - Rackets, paddles, and balls are located in the entryway cabinets
- Hiking
  - Reimers Ranch (20 min) has water, wildflowers, and rolling hills
  - Zilker Park and Barton Creek Greenbelt (30min, downtown Austin) are popular areas and have water features and lots of shade on the trails
  - Perdenales State Park (30 min) need to make a reservation, outstanding trails
- Horseback Riding
  - Trail ride at XLR8 Ranch
- Rock Climbing
  - Schedule 2+ weeks ahead with Sam Brodnax at Texas Climbing Adventures, at Reimers Ranch, great experience. [info@texasclimbingadventures.com](mailto:info@texasclimbingadventures.com) or <Copy>512-590-2988</Copy>

Drinking

- Driftwood Estate Winery (5 min), good vibes, no kids or dogs allowed, average wine, great vibes, food on the weekends, good board game tables
- Deep Eddys Vodka (15 min), outdoor or indoor activity, flight of flavors or regular drinks, fun vibes, no food
- Subs Monkey Brewery (20 min), kid-friendly, beer and pizza

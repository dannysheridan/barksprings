## Thermostat & AC

- There is one AC/Heat system for the whole house.
- Use the fans in the bedrooms to adjust the temperature.

## Dishware & Cookware

- **Handwash:** All pots and pans, sheet pans, and wood utensils.
- All plates, bowls, cups, etc. are dishwasher safe.
- All ceramic mugs and dishware are microwave safe.

## Coffee

- We have a Nespresso machine. Please refill the water tank before use.
- Extra pods and a bag for used pods are in the cabinet below the machine.
- To use: lift level > insert pod round side down > press lever > hit brew.

## Trash & Recycling

- Recycling = cardboard, tin, glass, paper, #1 & #2 plastic only.
- Everything else = trash.
- If bins are full, place bag in the garage.

## Food

- Feel free to use pantry or freezer items. If you finish something, please replace it.

## Laundry

- Turn on the laundry room fan (switch) when using the machines to keep the room cool.
- Empty the lint from the dryer each time before use.

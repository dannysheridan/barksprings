The house isn't just dog-friendly, it's dog-invited.

## Supplies & Equipment

- Dog bed, water bowls, and food bowls are located in the storage closet through the laundry room. You can also find treats and toys there.

## Bathroom Rules

- Dogs should use the bathroom only in the pea gravel areas. There is one pea gravel area in the front yard and one in the back yard.

- Please pick up after your dog and dispose of waste in the trash before leaving.

## Clean-up & Hygiene

- If your dog's paws are muddy, please use the outdoor shower to clean them before entering the house.

- A towel for the dog is located in the primary bathroom linen cabinet.

- Dog shampoo can be found in the storage closet through the laundry room.

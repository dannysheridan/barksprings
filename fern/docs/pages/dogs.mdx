The house isn't just dog-friendly, it's dog-invited.

## Supplies & Equipment

- Dog bed and water/food bowls are in the storage closet through the laundry room. You can also find treats and toys there.

## Bathroom Rules

- Dogs should go to the bathroom only in the pea gravel areas. There is one in the front and one in the back.

- Please pick up poop and put it in the trash before leaving.

## Clean-up & Hygiene

- If paws are muddy, please use the outdoor shower to clean them before entering.

- A towel for the dog is located in the primary bath linen cabinet.

- Dog shampoo can be found in the storage closet through the laundry room.
